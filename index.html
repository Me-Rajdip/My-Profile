About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated MSc Computer Science student at Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, specializing in data analysis and machine learning. My research focuses on federated learning for privacy-preserving AI, particularly in healthcare applications.

With a strong academic background in computer science and mathematics, I am constantly seeking opportunities to apply my skills to real-world problems. My work combines theoretical knowledge with practical implementation, always with an emphasis on data privacy and security.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I actively engage in research collaborations and continuously expand my expertise through certifications and hands-on projects. My goal is to contribute to innovative solutions that leverage AI while respecting user privacy.
