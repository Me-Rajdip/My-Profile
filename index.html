About Me

For people who know me, a personal description becomes unnecessary. For people who do not know me, I am a constant learner with a passion for acquiring knowledge. I possess a Master of Science in Computer Science from Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, specializing in data science, machine learning, and federated learning for privacy-preserving healthcare artificial intelligence.
